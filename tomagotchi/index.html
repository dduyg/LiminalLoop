Our Process

1. Discovery

To create a website that engages your unique audience, we must familiarize ourselves with the mission, culture, challenges, and objectives of your organization. This phase also provides an opportunity for our teams to get acquainted and set expectations around collaboration. Typically, the outcome of this phase is a Sitemap that dictates the architecture and content model of the website.

2. Design

This phase involves crafting the visual and interactive aspects of your website. Our design team will create wireframes, prototypes, and finalize the UI/UX design to ensure your site reflects your brand and resonates with your audience.

3. Handoff

Once the design is complete, we move into the handoff stage. Here, all design assets and documentation are handed over to the development team, ensuring a seamless transition into the build phase.

Pricing

We charge a flat project fee after agreeing on a detailed scope and timeline. Engagements typically cost between $40,000 to $175,000 and can take anywhere from 6 to 25 weeks depending on the complexity of the project.
